1. What Are Cookies

1.1: Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, mobile device, or other device when you visit a website or use certain online services.

1.3: Similar technologies, including software development kits (SDKs), pixels, local storage, web beacons, and device identifiers, may also be used within the Services. For the purposes of this Policy, these technologies are collectively referred to as "Cookies."
